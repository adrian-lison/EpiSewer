# Calculate the mean of a truncated normal distribution (truncated below zero) with mean mu and standard deviation sigma.

Calculate the mean of a truncated normal distribution (truncated below
zero) with mean mu and standard deviation sigma.

## Usage

``` r
trunc_normal_mean(mu, sigma)
```

## Arguments

- mu:

  Mean of the untruncated normal distribution

- sigma:

  Standard deviation of the untruncated normal distribution

## Value

The mean of the truncated normal distribution
